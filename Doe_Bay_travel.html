Getting Here

Travelling to Doe Bay is Easy

There are several ways to arrive. Most people take the ferry from Anacortes and drive here. Some take the opportunity to fly into Orcas Island. Some take a water taxi and bicycle.

Make a Ferry Reservation: Reservations can be made on line Go to Save A Spot. Or by calling 888-808-7977. As a courtesy, we will automatically send you a reminder to make your ferry reservation just prior to the release date for vehicle reservations.
